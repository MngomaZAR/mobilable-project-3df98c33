# Papzi Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-05

Papzi ("we", "us", "our") provides a marketplace for photography services. This policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Data we collect

- Account data: email, profile details, role, verification status.
- Marketplace data: bookings, messages, posts, comments, likes.
- Device and usage data: app diagnostics, error events, timestamps.
- Location data: precise or approximate location when enabled by you.

## How we use data

- Deliver core features (authentication, bookings, chat, feed, map discovery).
- Improve reliability, security, and abuse prevention.
- Respond to support requests and legal obligations.

## Data sharing

- We use infrastructure providers (for example Supabase) to operate the service.
- We do not sell personal data.
- We may disclose data when required by law or to protect users and the platform.

## Data retention

- We retain data while your account is active and for a limited period afterwards as required for legal, fraud, and operational purposes.
- Backups may persist for a limited additional retention period.

## Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may request:

- Access to your data.
- Correction of inaccurate data.
- Deletion of your account and related personal data.
- Restriction or objection to processing in limited circumstances.

To request account/data deletion, contact: support@papzi.co.za

## Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, but no system is guaranteed to be 100% secure.

## Children

Papzi is not intended for children under the minimum digital consent age in your region.

## Contact

Privacy inquiries: support@papzi.co.za
